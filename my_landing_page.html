LUNELLE

Lunelle is a breath of calm elegance—where cool aquatic notes meet soft iris and a hint of white tea. Wrapped in a tranquil blue aura, it’s crafted for those who move with grace and speak through presence. A scent that whispers sophistication.

ROSELLE

Roselle blooms with the sweetness of rose petals, lychee, and a touch of vanilla cream. Feminine yet spirited, it’s a celebration of charm and warmth. Think of it as a love letter in scent form—soft, joyful, and unforgettable.

VIORELLE

Viorelle unfolds like a twilight dream—velvety violet, plum, and smoky sandalwood. It’s a scent for the imaginative soul, wrapped in intrigue and allure. Deep, expressive, and unapologetically unique.
